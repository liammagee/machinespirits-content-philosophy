# Social Science Research and AI

Welcome to the Bolashak Program course on Social Science Research and AI. This course examines how artificial intelligence tools can augment traditional social science research methods while maintaining rigorous academic standards.

## Course Overview

This course covers:

- Introduction to AI distinctions and local AI tools (Ollama)
- Qualitative research methods with AI assistance
- Data analysis techniques using AI
- Cyber-social research approaches
- CAQDAS tools (Dedoose) for systematic analysis
- Mixed methods research design
- Academic publishing and journals
- Critical perspectives on AI and scholarship
- Generative AI capabilities and limitations

## Weekly Structure

Each week combines theoretical foundations with hands-on practice using AI tools for research tasks.
