Let’s Simulate an Assessment Workflow

We’ll create a little drama: “A Midsummer Night’s Assessment”
4 characters (Claude created these names):
“The Rubric Designer” (Dr. Sylvia Criterion)
“The Teacher” (Professor Jack Patience)
“The Student” (Maya Wisdom-Seeker)
“The Education Administrator” (Dean Victoria Metrix)
Setting: A modern university.
Plot: Standards are slipping. The administration feels teachers are lazy and students no longer care. Enter the hero: “The Rubric Designer”. Can they save the university from doom, despair… and endless low grades?

Slide 4

Workflow

Designer Creates the Rubric (version 1)
Teacher Develops an Assignment that Applies the Rubric
Student Writes an Essay
Administrator Reviews the Rubric, Assignment and Essay
Designer Refines the Rubric (version 2)
We (the Drama Critics!) Compare and Contrast Rubrics Versions 1 & 2
